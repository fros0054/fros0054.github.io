I did this in order to reach my goal, to teach english in Japan.

Teaching englihs in Japan is a massive goal in my life, I also want to travel and meet the woman of my dreams. So many things I would love to do. In order to get to Japan I need to first obtain a full bachelors degree. This will allow me get into the JET Programm (Japan Exchange Teacher's Programm) This will get me placed in a school and fly me over there free of charge. It would be a dream to teach in Japan and get to experience the culture and learn a new language. That pretty much sums up my life and where I want to get to. Thanks for reading :)
